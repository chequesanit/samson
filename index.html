What We Believe

Belief in God as Father, Son and Holy Spirit is at the heart of our faith.
Christians believe that Jesus is God's Son.

Jesus Is lord

Jesus Christ is Lord?For Jesus to be the Lord of your life
He has to be the boss of your whole life.

God Is love

God's love is unconditional; his love is very different
from human love,that is based on feeling.

The spirit Empowers

The Spirit empowers us by peeling away our sinful ways
and replacing them with godly characteristics.
